# WELDER — Summary of Duties & Tools/Equipment

---

## PART 1: Summary of Duties

- Preparation, layout, set-up, and fit-up of materials and equipment for welding operations.
- Perform combination welding using SMAW (Stick/MMA), GMAW (MIG), GTAW (TIG), FCAW, and laser welding processes on carbon steel, stainless steel, mild steel, alloy, aluminum, GI, and BI metals in all positions (1–6F and 1–6G) on plates, pipes, beams, tubular, purlins, and metal sheets of any thickness.
- Perform tacking, root pass, filling/multiple pass, and capping on pipes, plates, I-beams, H-beams, and structural components.
- Fabricate materials and complex parts/assemblies to support manufacturing and construction processes.
- Read and interpret drawings, key plans, and blueprints; perform layout and marking of materials.
- Drill, cut, rivet, bolt, and assemble metal components using hand and power tools per customer and build specifications.
- Fit and assemble metal components on production and assembly lines in accordance with project specifications.
- Design and fabricate custom welding projects as required.
- Conduct pre-inspection, in-process inspection, and final inspection of welded and finished products.
- Coordinate with assigned engineers and supervisors for turn-over and final inspection of completed work.
- Report and document used materials and consumables.
- Repair and rectify weld defects as identified during inspection.
- Conduct toolbox talks, discuss safety matters and concerns, and deploy team activities.
- Conduct task launch meetings for safe and correct work procedures based on approved Method Statements.
- Inspect power supply, equipment, accessories, and power tools prior to daily work; provide isolation, illumination, signage, and tagging for high-risk activities; request work permits.
- Monitor and supervise team workers and work activities on-site.
- Secure property housekeeping and proper storage of materials, equipment, and consumables before and after work.
- Ensure a safe workplace across structural, mid-rise, high-rise, condominium, hotel, expressway, and railway construction projects.
